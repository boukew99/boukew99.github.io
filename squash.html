Squash Game

Plays as a mysterious jumper, racing for his life to cross flags and other things to be on time.

Features

  • Jumping.
  • Pixel art with limited palette.
  • Unique design.

Story

In an underground base, plants have started growing on the edges of walls. This surfaced many strange creatures normally residing in the ground. One of these creatures drops a blank poncho wavering as the wind in a windstill cave. What forces does this poncho hold for our jumper?
