Pixel Icons & symbols/bit/one-bit

Icons of resolution of 16x16 px mostly only one color, which makes it binary and modulatable. This is the minimal context to make icons work, creating a possibility space of 2^(16*16)=1,157920892×10⁷⁷ images. A symbol is an familiar instance of an abstract concept.

Usage

The difference between icon and symbols?.

  2. Save Image As... on the image by right-clicking on it.
  3. Optionally Repeat from 1

Effective Emoji's usage

Emoji originally came in the form of Emoticons to form an image with characters rather than a word. Example are: :), :( or more complex ones such as (^-^*). These communicate subtler emotions, which has no other convenient or accesible way to communicate in words. Usally we would use body language for this in natural conversation, but chatting mechanics are more constrained.
