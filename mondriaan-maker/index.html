Mondriaan Maker project

A creative program with a minimal user interface.

Inception

Before Mondriaan Maker there was resizable rectangles. It was a fixed size of 3 by 3 rectangles each expanding, but their edges resizable. The next step was adding colors to the rectangles. By accident I made something that reminded me of an Mondriaan painting, and so I applied an mondriaan palette. Then came the infinite rectangles update, scaling up the possibilities of programm. It took some time to polish the interaction, but the coloring bar could be replaced by mutating colors and color dragging. The screenshot button could be replaced by an timed screenshot popup.
